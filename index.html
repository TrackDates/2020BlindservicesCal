2020 Measure

Digital Measure Sheet (Leica DISTO X3)

Use Leica Bluetooth keyboard mode to “type” measurements straight into the form. Works on Windows/iPad/Android without special Bluetooth APIs.

Open Measure Form Admin Console
Setup (pairing + best settings)
  1. Pair the DISTO X3 to your device in Bluetooth settings (it will show as a keyboard).
  2. On the X3: in Bluetooth settings, set termination to Tab so it jumps to the next field.
  3. Optional: turn units transfer off so it sends plain numbers (easier to parse).

Offline-friendly

The form keeps a draft locally (per job number) so you don’t lose work on site.
